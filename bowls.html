Bowls

Add a touch of artistry to your kitchen with these beautiful handmade bowls. Each bowl is thrown and glazed to order, ensuring that you get a unique piece every time. You'll be struck by the beautiful color and texture of each bowl and will love serving friends and family in them. Stoneware is a timeless and durable material, well-loved for bowls, plates, and mugs, and we know you'll love admiring the work we do with ours!
These bowls are made from white stoneware and take roughly 1-2 weeks to complete. Please keep this in mind if you are planning for a special occasion. If you need your bowl sooner please message us and we can try to work something out.
All our pieces are made in our small shop in Douglasville, Georgia. Each piece is thrown and glazed individually, therefore, they will be similar in size, shape, and color, but natural variations will occur. That's what makes handmade pieces so special!
