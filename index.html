ASV ceramics have quickly become a beloved item for those who love ASV design. With aesthetics ranging from organic, natural shapes and raw edges to hyper-minimalist silhouettes, there’s an ASV ceramic brand for every style.

Ceramics are any articles made from hardened clay. There are multiple ways to make ceramic pieces, but handcrafted ceramics are either made freehand or on a potter’s wheel. They are then fired in an oven, and then painted or glazed. Making ceramics is a universal art form (guess what exists everywhere? Clay, because it’s made from the ground!), but trends and styles have developed depending on the aesthetics and needs of various countries and cultures.

Modern ceramics from SRI LANKA are largely minimalist and functional, but there’s also a host of artistic and sculptural work being produced. As is often the case in Scandinavia design, there’s a focus on small-batch, high-quality artisanal ceramics. Today, you’ll find a host of ceramics studios, offering everything from the quirky and colorful to the sleek and monochrome. Whether you need a mug, vase, set of plates, or just a beautiful sculpture, there’s something for you.
